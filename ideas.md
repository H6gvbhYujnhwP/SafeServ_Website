## CHOSEN APPROACH: Cinematic Deep Space

The Cinematic Deep Space aesthetic perfectly matches SafeServ's positioning as a premium, forward-thinking technology partner. The deep navy darkness communicates trust and reliability, while the vibrant teal signals innovation and connectivity. The motion-forward design will deliver the "wow factor" James wants for website visitors.

**Committed design tokens:**
- Background: #0D1B2A (Deep Navy)
- Primary Accent: #2DD4BF (Vibrant Teal)
- Secondary Accent: #F59E0B (Warm Gold — CTAs only)
- Text Primary: #FFFFFF
- Text Secondary: #94A3B8 (Slate)
- Section Alt BG: #0F2336 (Slightly lighter navy)
